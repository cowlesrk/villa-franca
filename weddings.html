A day in the Villa Franca...

Sweet scents and the sounds of nature saturate the air in the stately gardens of Villa Franca Estate. From formal hedges meticulously groomed to the sweet smell of our 79 rose bushes on the grounds it is a magical setting. Framed by hundreds of acres of lush farm fields, miniature lights dance among the trees making it a setting like no other. You and your guests will enjoy a leisure stroll around the grounds whether it be afternoon or as the the beautiful sun sets just beyond the Rockwall gardens of the Villa Franca Estate.
