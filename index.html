Where Vintage Elegance meets Southern Charm

Looking for a venue for your wedding in the Concord, Mooresville, China Grove or Charlotte area? Need help trying to find a place in North Carolina with a beautiful natural setting, but yet, with all the modern-day accommodations required to make your special event a success? If so, you have come to the right place. The Villa Franca Estate offers many amenities to make your wedding or special event unforgettable

Beautiful Gardens

With 4 acres of beautifully planted and meticulously-maintained garden areas, and hundreds of acres of cultivated fields, the Villa Franca is a place of quiet retreat, where you can still see the stars at night and the air is filled with soothing sounds of nature.

Enjoy Nature

The Villa Franca is surrounded by gorgeous farmland and rolling pastures. We are conveniently located in China Grove, NC which is in the outskirts of Concord, NC. When selecting our venue, a petting zoo with real farm animals is available for enjoyment of both children and adults. Incorporate nature into your event with the Villa Franca.

Stress-Free Planning

Many of our venue packages include chairs, tables, linens, tents and many other features for your convenience. This means that you will spend less time worrying about renting these and more time enjoying the planning of your wedding or special event.
